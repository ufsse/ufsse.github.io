What Is The Society Of Sales Engineers?

The Society of Sales Engineers is an organization that focuses on building a member base that possesses both the technical skills to solve complex problems and the interpersonal skills to interface with customers, management, and colleagues every day. Our members are not afraid to approach people and not afraid to take on a challenge. We encourage our members to step out of their comfort zone, reach out to companies, and seek jobs for top companies in areas such as Technical Sales, Marketing, or Consulting.

SSE was founded in Fall 2011 by five Industrial Engineering students who saw a need for an organization geared toward helping outgoing engineers get jobs well aligned with their personalities and interests. Since then, the organization has grown to over 70 members and continues to grow every semester. Over time, we have developed a solid foundation of affiliated companies that includes Trane, Siemens, Parker Hannifin, Johnson Controls, Nielsen, Citrix, Certainty 3D, and we are reaching out to new companies on a regular basis to give them the opportunity to get involved. Our general body meetings are a great way for company representatives to meet our members and officers and to take resumes for potential candidates.

If you are a company representative or a potential member looking to get involved with us, please feel free to reach out to our current President, Michael Romano, at Mike.Romano@ufsse.com.
